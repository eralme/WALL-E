## Textbooks and Online Books

### Digital Logic and Computer Architecture

**Free Resources:**
- **Digital Design and Computer Architecture** - David Harris & Sarah Harris
  - Excellent intro to digital logic and RISC-V architecture
  - Available: University libraries, purchasing options

- **The Elements of Computing Systems (Nand2Tetris)** - Noam Nisan & Shimon Schocken
  - Build a computer from first principles
  - Website: https://www.nand2tetris.org/
  - Free course materials and book companion

- **Introduction to Computing Systems** - Yale Patt & Sanjay Patel
  - From bits to C programming
  - Companion to course materials

- **Computer Organization and Design** (RISC-V Edition) - Patterson & Hennessy
  - The classic computer architecture textbook
  - RISC-V edition is more beginner-friendly

**Open Access:**
- **The Art of Assembly Language** - Randall Hyde
  - Free online: https://www.plantation-productions.com/Webster/
  - Deep dive into x86 assembly

### Electronics and Circuit Theory

**Free Resources:**
- **Lessons in Electric Circuits** - Tony Kuphaldt
  - Free online textbook series
  - Website: https://www.allaboutcircuits.com/textbook/
  - Covers DC, AC, semiconductors, digital circuits

- **Practical Electronics for Inventors** - Paul Scherz & Simon Monk
  - Comprehensive electronics reference
  - Available: Libraries, for purchase

- **The Art of Electronics** - Horowitz & Hill
  - Industry standard reference (commercial)
  - Expensive but comprehensive

**Open Access:**
- **Circuit Analysis For Dummies** - John Santiago
  - Beginner-friendly introduction

### Embedded Systems and Microcontrollers

**Free Resources:**
- **Arduino Cookbook** - Michael Margolis
  - Practical recipes for Arduino projects

- **Mastering the Raspberry Pi** - Warren Gay
  - Deep dive into Pi hardware

- **Programming Arduino** - Simon Monk
  - Getting started with Arduino programming

- **Making Embedded Systems** - Elecia White
  - Professional embedded development practices
  - Available: Libraries, for purchase

**Open Access:**
- **AVR Programming** - Elliot Williams
  - Free online materials
  - Deep dive into AVR microcontrollers

- **Discovering the STM32** - Geoffrey Brown
  - Free PDF available online
  - ARM Cortex-M programming

### C/C++ Programming

**Free Resources:**
- **The C Programming Language** - Kernighan & Ritchie (K&R)
  - The definitive C book
  - Worth purchasing

- **C++ Primer** - Stanley Lippman
  - Comprehensive C++ introduction

**Open Access:**
- **Learn C the Hard Way** - Zed Shaw
  - Free online: https://learncodethehardway.com/
  - Hands-on approach

- **Beej's Guide to C Programming**
  - Free online: https://beej.us/guide/bgc/
  - Excellent beginner resource

## Learning Strategies

### How to Use These Resources

1. **Don't try to consume everything** - These are references, not requirements
2. **Use resources when stuck** - Reference docs and forums when you need help
3. **Watch videos for reinforcement** - If a lesson is unclear, find a video on the topic
4. **Build projects** - Apply what you learn immediately
5. **Join communities** - Ask questions, share progress, help others

### Recommended Supplementary Schedule

- **Daily**: Check Hackaday for project inspiration
- **Weekly**: Watch 1-2 videos from recommended channels
- **As Needed**: Reference documentation and calculators
- **When Stuck**: Search forums, Stack Exchange, or ask community
- **For Deep Dives**: Read relevant textbook chapters
